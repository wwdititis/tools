    HOW TO PLAY

    Guess the name of our OFMD
    cast & crew in 6 tries:
    1. Type in your guess
    2. Each guess must be a 5 letter word relevant to the OFMD universe
    3. Hit the enter button to submit
    After each guess, the color of the
    tiles will change to show how close
    your guess was to the right word.

    EXAMPLES
    Letters in pink are in the word
    and in the correct spot

    Letters in orange are in the word
    but in the wrong spot

    Letters in brown are not in
    the word in any spot
